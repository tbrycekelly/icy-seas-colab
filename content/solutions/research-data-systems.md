# Research data systems

Scientific datasets evolve through collection, correction, interpretation, collaboration, and publication. We design systems that preserve that history while helping a team move toward a trustworthy, reusable release.

## What we help solve

- Project data architecture and workflow design
- Metadata schemas, documentation, and readiness review
- File versioning, reconciliation, and provenance
- Databases, portals, APIs, and project dashboards
- Permissions, audit history, and collaborator handoffs
- Archive preparation, release packaging, and reproducibility review

## A typical engagement

We begin by mapping how data enter the project, where decisions are currently recorded, who needs access, and what the final deliverables require. From there we define a practical architecture, pilot it with representative data, document the workflow, and support the team through adoption or delivery.
