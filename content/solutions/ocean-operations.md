# Ocean operations

Shipboard and remote work demand systems that remain understandable when bandwidth, power, time, and attention are limited. We connect operational events, instrument data, positions, processing decisions, and post-cruise delivery without assuming continuous internet access.

## Capabilities

- Offline-first event, station, and sequence logging
- GPS, NMEA, serial, TCP, and UDP instrument integration
- CTD ingest, processing, comparison, and export
- Field dashboards and system-health monitoring
- Local databases, backups, and resilient deployment planning
- Post-cruise reconciliation and data handoff
- Configuration, operator training, and field support

## From planning through handoff

An ocean-operations engagement can begin with cruise workflow design, proceed through configuration and hardware integration, include a controlled field pilot, and end with documented exports and a post-mission review. The objective is not simply to collect more data—it is to preserve enough context to use those data confidently afterward.
