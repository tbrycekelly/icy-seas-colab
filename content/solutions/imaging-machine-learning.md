# Imaging & machine learning

Large image collections become scientifically useful only when detection, context, measurements, human judgment, model behavior, and quality control remain connected. We build workflows that reduce review burden without turning the analysis into an opaque black box.

## Capabilities

- Image and video ingestion architecture
- Preprocessing, segmentation, detection, and ROI refinement
- Human review, annotation, labeling, and quality control
- Training-dataset construction and mask editing
- Classification and segmentation model development
- CPU, GPU, and distributed worker systems
- Reproducible exports for downstream analysis

## Built around scientific review

The appropriate balance of automation and review depends on the instrument, image quality, target organisms or particles, and intended analysis. We begin with representative imagery and the desired scientific record, then design the processing and review path around those constraints.
