# Scientific consulting

Not every research problem needs a new product. We provide focused technical capacity for projects that need an experienced scientific partner, a defined deliverable, or help connecting several parts of a workflow.

## Engagement areas

- Statistical, spatial, time-series, and environmental data analysis
- Biogeochemical and oceanographic modeling
- Scientific software, databases, APIs, and dashboards
- Data management, metadata, and archive delivery
- Laboratory and field-system design
- Software, methods, and project reviews
- Proposals, manuscripts, reports, and technical documentation
- Training, mentorship, lectures, and seminars

## Systems available through engagements

[Baseline by Icy Seas](/product/baseline/) can support pilot laboratory-monitoring deployments. Datum, FixPoint, Pelagia, castx, and Technology Lab components can also be configured or extended when they fit the project need; they are tools within the engagement rather than a requirement.

## Clear scope and delivery

We can work through hourly consulting, a fixed scope of work, a product pilot, a subcontract or subaward, or longer-term project support. Each engagement defines responsibilities, assumptions, milestones, documentation, and delivery expectations before work begins.
