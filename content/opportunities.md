# Employment Opportunities

Icy Seas Co-Laboratory is a small but growing collective of scientists, developers, and educators dedicated to advancing open, Arctic-focused science. Our work blends data analysis, ocean modeling, visualization, and outreach—rooted in open-source tools and community collaboration.

We seek people who are curious, self-motivated, and committed to transparent, reproducible research. Whether you’re a data scientist fluent in Python or Julia, an R developer, a visualization specialist, or an Arctic researcher eager to bridge field data with digital tools, we’d love to hear from you.

---

## Current Openings

**There are no formal openings at this time**, but we regularly collaborate with students, independent contractors, and research partners on short-term and project-based work.  

If you’re interested in contributing to our projects, please reach out with your background and interests—we often develop new roles around great ideas and the right people.

---

## How to Apply

To express interest in future opportunities, email:  **[tkelly@icyseascolab.io](mailto:tkelly@icyseascolab.io)**  

Include a short description of your skills, interests, and how you’d like to contribute. Links to GitHub repositories, datasets, publications, or personal projects are encouraged.

---

## Our Values

We believe in open science, flexibility, and respect for both data and people. Our workplace—whether in Fairbanks, remote, or at sea—values collaboration, curiosity, and autonomy.  

Icy Seas Co-Lab supports equitable hiring and welcomes applicants from all backgrounds who share our vision for accessible and community-driven Arctic science.
