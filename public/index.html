About

At Icy Seas Co-Laboratory LLC, we bring together science, technology, and collaboration to better understand our changing oceans. Founded in Fairbanks, Alaska, we specialize in oceanographic research, data-driven solutions, and scientific consulting.

Mission

Our work bridges field observations, advanced computing, and clear communication—helping researchers, communities, and organizations make informed decisions in a rapidly shifting Arctic.

Approach

We combine environmental observational systems, modeling tools, and creativity to diagnose processes in the earth climate system. This work bridges both discplines and scales.
